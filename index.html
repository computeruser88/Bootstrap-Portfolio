About Me

A customer service worker by necessity, I am a graduate of Penn State University with a Master's Degree in Software Engineering. Currently I attend the Coding Boot Camp at UCSD Extension with aspirations of becoming a web developer.

In my spare time I have competed in the USAPL as a Masters 1 powerlifter and twice won second place in the California State Games: as a 74kg lifter in 2015, and as an 83kg lifter in 2016. I have a passion for many musical genres and also dabbled in amateur music creation when I was younger.
